**Subject:** Vizcom ships Smart Dropper

Vizcom today launched Smart Dropper, a reference-image style transfer tool that lets industrial designers apply the visual language of any image — material, color, surface, mood — to a sketch or render without re-prompting. The feature reduces the moodboard-to-render translation step for product, footwear, and consumer-electronics design teams, and works inside Workbench alongside Collections, Modify, and Instant Render. Sophia Silver, Product Marketing Manager at Vizcom, is available for comment; full announcement at https://vizcom.com/blog.
