A reference says more than a prompt.

Smart Dropper lets you point Vizcom at any image — a retro Mac, a tamagotchi, your favorite album cover — and pull its visual language straight onto your design. Material, color, surface, mood. The form you sketched stays intact; the vibe shifts to match what you pointed at.

The references on your moodboard already say what you want. Smart Dropper skips the translation step. It lives inside Workbench alongside Collections, Modify, and Instant Render, so the workflow stays connected — drop a reference, target a sketch, keep going.

Available today for all users in Vizcom.

The image was always the brief. Now it can be the input.
