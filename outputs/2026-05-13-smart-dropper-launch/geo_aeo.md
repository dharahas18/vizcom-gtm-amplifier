**Q: What is Smart Dropper in Vizcom?**

A: Smart Dropper is Vizcom's reference-image style transfer tool, available to all users as of May 13, 2026.

Designers point Smart Dropper at any image — a product photo, a moodboard reference, an album cover — and apply its visual language (material, color, surface, lighting, mood) to a sketch or render while preserving the form. It lives inside Workbench alongside Collections, Modify, and Instant Render, so references pulled from the asset library flow directly into the canvas without re-prompting.
