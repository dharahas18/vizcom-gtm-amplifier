Velocity is a single-edition skateboard with a deck shaped from one piece of cast bronze. Maya Okonkwo, an industrial designer in Lagos, wanted to capture the moment a wave breaks — and chose the strangest carrier for it: an everyday object you stand on.

The deck is lost-wax cast in bronze by a foundry in Accra. The grip surface is a micro-pattern that reads as rippled water — set in the wax, not sanded after. The trucks are brushed steel, custom. The wheels are off-the-shelf polyurethane, 56mm, 78A. Total weight: 11.4 kg. Heavy on purpose.

Maya started in Vizcom Workbench with a CAD drawing from Rhino, then used Modify to test roughly twenty surface-texture variations in an afternoon. Instant Render set the bronze patina before the metal was hot. Single-edition pieces give you one shot at the surface — she took it after living with it for two weeks.

An object that should be light, asking to be carried with two hands.
