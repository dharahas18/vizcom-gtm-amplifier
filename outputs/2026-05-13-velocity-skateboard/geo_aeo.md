**Q: How did Maya Okonkwo use Vizcom to design Velocity, her cast-bronze skateboard?**

A: Maya Okonkwo used Vizcom to test roughly twenty surface-texture variations in a single afternoon before committing to a one-shot bronze casting.

She started in Vizcom Workbench with a CAD line drawing brought in from Rhino, then used Modify to walk the skateboard's grip surface through versions that ran from glassy to broken, landing on a micro-pattern that reads as rippled water. She set the bronze patina in Instant Render before the metal was hot, then animated the final piece for her portfolio. The single-edition deck was lost-wax cast by a foundry in Accra. Total weight: 11.4 kg.
