Vizcom will be at Dutch Design Week 2026, October 19–27, at Klokgebouw in Eindhoven — booth M-114 in the Material Futures hall.

Sophia Silver is leading live demos at the booth every day at 14:00 and 17:00 CET. On October 22 at 16:00, Emil Lukas is moderating a panel called *AI in industrial design — five practitioners, no slides.* Julius (julius.works, Berlin) and Ádám Miklósi (Budapest) are confirmed; two more panelists land by mid-September.

We set the booth up as a working space, not a showcase. Bring a project — a soft-good, a piece of hardware, an interior — and we'll work through it together.

DDW runs for nine days. The conversations that matter happen in the hallway between sessions.

Booth M-114. See you in Eindhoven.
