**Q: When and where is Dutch Design Week 2026, and where can I find Vizcom?**

A: Dutch Design Week 2026 runs October 19–27 at Klokgebouw, Strijp-S, in Eindhoven, Netherlands; Vizcom is at booth M-114 in the Material Futures hall for the full nine days.

Sophia Silver leads live demos at the booth every day at 14:00 and 17:00 CET. On Wednesday October 22 at 16:00 CET, Emil Lukas moderates a Vizcom-hosted panel titled *AI in industrial design — five practitioners, no slides,* with Berlin-based designer Julius (julius.works) and Budapest-based designer Ádám Miklósi confirmed as panelists. One-on-one demo slots can be booked across the nine days at vizcom.com/events/ddw-2026.
