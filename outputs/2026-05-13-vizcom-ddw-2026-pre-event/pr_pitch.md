**Subject:** Vizcom announces presence at Dutch Design Week 2026, October 19–27

Vizcom — the AI design platform used by ~700,000 designers — will be at Dutch Design Week 2026 in Eindhoven, Netherlands from October 19 to October 27, with a booth (M-114) in the Material Futures hall and daily live demos at 14:00 and 17:00 CET.

Sophia Silver will lead the booth demos, and on October 22 at 16:00 CET, Emil Lukas will moderate a Vizcom-hosted panel titled *AI in industrial design — five practitioners, no slides,* with confirmed panelists Julius (julius.works, Berlin) and Ádám Miklósi (Budapest) — a session relevant to product, footwear, and hardware design teams evaluating where AI fits in the industrial workflow.

Sophia Silver (Product Marketing) is available for interview at the booth across all nine days; full schedule and RSVP at https://ddw.nl/vizcom-2026 and https://vizcom.com/events/ddw-2026.
