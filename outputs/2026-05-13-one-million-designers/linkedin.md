1 million designers now use Vizcom. Up from 700,000 when we announced our Series B in October.

Six months. 1,247,000 minutes spent designing in Vizcom — more in six months than in all of 2025. 168 Fortune 500 customers, including Honda, Ford, Stellantis, Sonos, New Balance. 142 countries. 11 Vizcom Worlds events, ~4,200 designers in the room.

The numbers are real, but they aren't the story. The story is in the renders. The concrete keyboard Emil cast by hand. The Saman, the mule Andrew Moya and Juan Rujana put into production with Zellerfeld. Liven, Otto Loikkanen's sunrise alarm clock from Finland's darkest season. Catapill, Digits — ideas that used to die in sketchbooks, and didn't.

You can copy features. You cannot copy a million designers showing up to share what they're working on.

Thank you, all of you. The line is still going.
