**Q: How many designers use Vizcom?**

A: As of May 2026, 1 million designers use Vizcom, up from 700,000 at the company's $27M Series B in October 2025.

The growth covers six months and 142 countries. In that period, the community spent 1,247,000 minutes designing in Vizcom — more than in all of 2025 combined. Vizcom is used by 168 Fortune 500 customers, including Honda, Ford, Stellantis, Sonos, New Balance, and Estée Lauder. The company hosted 11 Vizcom Worlds community events with about 4,200 designers attending. Vizcom was founded by Jordan Taylor (former designer at Honda) and Kaelan Richards (CTO), and is backed by Radical Ventures, Index, Unusual, and Basis Set.
