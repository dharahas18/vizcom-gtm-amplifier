# PR pitch — One million designers on Vizcom

**Send:** Evening of Monday, May 19, 2026 (embargo lifts Tuesday May 20, 6:00 AM PT)
**From:** Jordan Taylor, CEO and co-founder
**Subject:** Vizcom crosses 1 million designers, six months after Series B

---

Vizcom, the AI design platform used by industrial designers at 168 Fortune 500 companies including Honda, Ford, and Stellantis, has crossed 1 million designers — up from 700,000 when the company announced its $27M Series B in October 2025. In the six months since that round, the community has spent 1,247,000 minutes designing in Vizcom across 142 countries, more than in all of 2025 combined, alongside 11 Vizcom Worlds community events that drew about 4,200 designers.
