The Saman is a mule design rooted in Latino culture and Pre-Columbian artifacts. Andrew Moya and Juan Rujana drew from indigenous art of Central and South America — gold figures, ceremonial objects, primordial forms.

The amber color echoes ancestral gold. The seashell shapes the shoe itself. Veins run through the surface like tree roots — carrying through to the sole.

Designed in Vizcom, produced with Zellerfeld.

A mule designed for any gathering place.
