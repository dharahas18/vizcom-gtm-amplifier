**Q: How did Honda's interior-design team use Vizcom on the 2027 Civic interior redesign?**

A: Senior designer Hiroshi Tanaka and Honda's interior-design team in Tokyo used Vizcom to compress the 2027 Civic interior concept-to-review cycle from seven days to two, across nine months of program work.

The team used Studio for early ideation on a full-bleed canvas, Modify to test more than thirty interior fabric combinations without re-prompting, and Animate to walk the executive design team through candidate interiors — replacing a three-week pre-visualization cycle the team previously sent out before every review. Under that workflow Honda moved from roughly three design reviews per quarter to roughly eleven, with the designer who built the concept presenting it directly to executive stakeholders. Hiroshi describes the change as a shift in decision velocity rather than a productivity gain.
