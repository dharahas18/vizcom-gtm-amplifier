## 1. About Vizcom (facts the agent may use without confirmation)

- **What it is.** Vizcom is an AI design platform for industrial designers. Sketch-to-photorealistic-render in seconds.
- **Scale.** ~700,000 designers; 150+ companies. Named customers and confirmed partners: see `core_rules.md` Section 6.
- **Funding.** $27M Series B closed October 2025, led by Radical Ventures with Index Ventures, Unusual Ventures, Basis Set, and angels continuing.
- **Founders.** Jordan Taylor (CEO, ex-Honda industrial designer, ex-NVIDIA). Kaelan Richards (CTO).
- **Founding lineage.** Predecessor was Designerspen, an Instagram community Jordan Taylor started in 2020 (~80k followers, "Feeding your curiosity with Art and Design"). It is **not** Vizcom's brand channel — see `core_rules.md` Section 8.
- **Stated mission.** "Redefine how design takes shape by building AI-powered tools that accelerate the creative process from sketch to substance" (Feb 2024).
- **What Vizcom names itself.** A platform / a creative-workflow tool / a co-pilot for industrial design. Never "the AI that designs your products."

## 2. Voice pillars expanded — with corpus evidence

(Compact form in `core_rules.md` Section 1. Below: the *why*.)

### Pillar 1 — Designer-first, tool-second
In every creator story (Samples 1, 3, 4, 5), the designer's name appears in the first 30 words and Vizcom is named later, with its role described as "sharpening the vision," "translating it into visual clarity," or "accelerating the middle phase." In product launches (Samples 2, 8, 11), features are framed by the designer pain they remove ("the render looks great, but now I need to keep working on it").

### Pillar 2 — Specific over abstract
Materials are named (stainless steel, solid birch, cork, matte off-white textured plastic, aluminum extrusions, concrete). Tools use feature-name capitalization. Numbers are exact (657,403 minutes, up to ten colors, up to eight items). Cities and studios are named (Berlin / Budapest / Finland / juliusworks.de / adammiklosi.com).

### Pillar 3 — Calm confidence over hype
Vizcom describes what it does and what it doesn't. "Available now." "Without leaving Vizcom." "Without re-prompting." Series B post: "AI will not replace designers. It will amplify them." Year in Review chooses gratitude over chest-beating.

### Pillar 4 — Channel-native
Same signal, four cadences. The canonical X-→-LinkedIn relationship: the LinkedIn post is the X tweet **with more room to breathe** — same designers, same materials, plus 1–2 reflective sentences and a standalone poetic closer. See Sample 12 T3 vs Sample 13 P1.

### Pillar 5 — Never invent
The corpus is full of specifics because Vizcom only writes what's true. Missing fact → `[NEEDS CONFIRMATION]` flag in `summary.md`.

### Pillar 6 — AI as extension
The through-line from the 2024 Mueller interview through the 2026 concrete-keyboard story.

## 3. Signal taxonomy — the 10 types

For the template mapping see `core_rules.md` Section 5. Below: the *definition* and channel emphasis per type.

### 3.1 customer_win
A named enterprise customer publicly adopts or talks about Vizcom. Validate against the allowed list (`core_rules.md` Section 6).
**Channel emphasis.** LinkedIn leads. PR pitch parallel.

### 3.2 product_launch
Vizcom ships a feature, product, or pricing change.
**Channel emphasis.** Blog leads. X amplifies. LinkedIn educates. GEO/AEO high-value.

### 3.3 event
Vizcom sponsoring / appearing at a conference, fair, studio takeover, webinar.
**Channel emphasis.** LinkedIn + X pre-event; Instagram during; blog post-event.

### 3.4 press_mention
A trade or mainstream publication writes about Vizcom.
**Channel emphasis.** LinkedIn + X. Blog only when the piece is substantial.

### 3.5 milestone
Funding round, customer-count threshold, anniversary, team expansion.
**Channel emphasis.** Blog leads. PR pitch parallel. LinkedIn + X.

### 3.6 ugc_moment
A designer publicly shares Vizcom-made work that hits a tipping point of attention.
**Channel emphasis.** Instagram + X lead. Blog only with permission for a full feature.

### 3.7 trend_response
An industry conversation Vizcom can credibly enter (e.g., "is AI replacing designers?", "the death of the portfolio").
**Channel emphasis.** Blog leads. LinkedIn + GEO/AEO high-value.

### 3.8 educator_activity
Vizcom for Educators activity — school adopts Vizcom, professor publishes curriculum, student work goes viral.
**Channel emphasis.** LinkedIn leads (education buyers). Blog + Instagram amplify.

### 3.9 community_moment
A Hold Your Ideas project, a Vizcom Challenge result, a Discord community story.
**Channel emphasis.** Blog leads. Instagram + X amplify. LinkedIn optional.

### 3.10 cultural_moment
A broader cultural moment Vizcom can connect to (major design exhibition, manufacturing-anniversary, craft-vs-AI debate).
**Channel emphasis.** Blog leads. LinkedIn + X.

## 4. Things to avoid — reasoning

The forbidden list lives in `core_rules.md` Section 2. Below: *why* each category is forbidden.

| Category | Why |
|---|---|
| Hype words | Corpus is clean of them. Vizcom uses verbs and numbers, not adjective stacks. |
| Replacement framing | Contradicts Pillar 6. The framing Vizcom explicitly rejects. |
| Direct competitor names | Vizcom never names competitors in its own voice. |
| 4+ hashtag stacks | UGC pattern (Sample 15), not Vizcom's. |
| Mythic / florid register | Reserved for manifesto (Sample 10's 2024 register). |
| Inventing facts | Hard rule. Flag `[NEEDS CONFIRMATION]`. |
| B2B clichés | Out of register. |
| Exclamation points outside cleared quotes | Vizcom's institutional voice is calm. |
| Bullet lists in creator stories | Creator stories are prose; bullets reserved for product-launch feature breakdowns, tables, `summary.md`. |

## 5. Voice patterns extracted from corpus (sentence-level moves)

### 5.1 Moves Vizcom uses
- **Verb-stack rhythm.** "Sketch it. Render it. Animate it." *(NOTE: not observed in 2026 X feed — deprecated as a default. Still useful on the blog for setup beats.)*
- **One-sentence paragraphs as punctuation.** "It works. Exceptionally well." (Sample 1.)
- **Em-dashes for in-line clarification.** "—but unlike existing solutions, Liven is designed to earn its place on the bedside table." (Sample 3.)
- **The contrarian hook.** "Most people see an exhaust pipe and think horsepower. Julius saw a candlelight holder." (Sample 5.)
- **The aphoristic opener.** "Design is a physical act." / "Most ideas die in sketchbooks." (Samples 1, 2.)
- **The "What if" stack.** Sample 7.
- **The "we've heard this from you" reframe.** Sample 2.
- **The credits footer.** Samples 3, 4, 5.

### 5.2 Vocabulary the agent should reach for
- speed of thought / speed of imagination
- sketch to substance / from idea to reality, faster
- hold your ideas / make it real
- material honesty / industrial honesty / tactile / resolved object
- the rhythm of creation / flow state / the messy middle of design
- creative orchestrator / creative fuel / creative capacity
- art-directing (verb) / refining / iterating / shaping
- co-pilot / collaborator / extension / partner / scaffolding

### 5.3 Tone calibration per channel

| Channel | Register |
|---|---|
| `blog.md` (creator story) | Editorial profile. Designer-as-protagonist. Em-dashes welcome. |
| `blog.md` (product launch) | Calm, operational. Verb-led subheads. Numbers ground claims. |
| `blog.md` (milestone) | Founder-letter. Personal "I/we." Aphoristic line acceptable. |
| `linkedin.md` | Education-stance. Designer/customer named first. |
| `x_thread.md` | Punchy, verb-led. Designer tagged. |
| `geo_aeo.md` | Documentation-style. Direct answer first. |
| `pr_pitch.md` | Trade-press neutral. No hype. Three sentences. |
| `summary.md` | Internal-memo. Bullets okay. |

## 6. Render handling

Every signal must reference at least one Vizcom render. Signal file specifies `hero_render` (path or URL) and optional `secondary_renders` (up to 2).

The agent embeds the hero_render in the blog body, in tweet 1, in the LinkedIn hero slot, and in the Instagram caption. The agent does not generate renders; it names them.

Missing or unreadable `hero_render` → halt and flag `[NEEDS CONFIRMATION: hero_render not provided]` in `summary.md`.
