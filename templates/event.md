# Template — Event

**signal_type =** `event`.

Vizcom sponsoring / appearing at / hosting a design conference, fair, studio takeover, webinar, or community meetup. Pre-event and post-event variants.

Channel-length / hashtag / emoji rules live in `core_rules.md`.

---

## Pre-event vs. post-event

| Mode | When | What |
|---|---|---|
| **Pre-event** | Days/weeks before | What we're showing, who's speaking, when/where, how to book a demo or RSVP |
| **Post-event** | Day-of or day-after | What we saw, designer spotlights, key conversations |

Signal frontmatter has `event_mode: pre|post`. If unclear, ask in intake.

---

## `blog.md`

### H1 patterns
- Pre: `Vizcom at {Event}: What we're bringing` / `Join us at {Event}: {one-line}`
- Post: `Notes from {Event}` / `What we saw at {Event}`

### Pre-event body H2 outline
```
[Hook — event, one paragraph. Date + place + scale.]

## What we're showing
## Who's speaking
## How to find us
## (optional) Book a demo
```

### Post-event body H2 outline
```
[Hook — specific moment. A designer conversation, a demo reaction.]

## The setting
## Conversations that mattered
(3–5 short paragraphs, each naming a specific designer / team / studio Vizcom met.)
## What we're taking away
## (optional) See more
```

### Closing
- Pre: `See you {there / at booth #} on {date}.`
- Post: `Thanks to everyone who came by.` (No exclamation point.)

## `linkedin.md` — opener formulas

- Pre: `We're going live with {partner / speaker} on {date}.` / `Vizcom will be at {Event} on {date}.`
- Post: `Notes from {Event}, {place}, {date}.`

Body: paragraph 1 = event details (pre) or most-cited conversation (post); paragraph 2 = value to attendees / takeaway.

Closer:
- Pre: clear RSVP / book-a-demo line with link.
- Post: a reflective line.

## `pr_pitch.md`

**Pre-event subject:** `Vizcom announces presence at {Event}, {date}`

**Pre-event three sentences:**
1. `Vizcom — the AI design platform used by ~700,000 designers — will be at {Event} in {place} from {start} to {end}.`
2. `{Vizcom speaker / what we're showing}, with sessions on {topic} relevant to {industry segment}.`
3. `{Contact} is available for interview; details at {event URL or blog URL}.`

**Post-event three sentences:**
1. `Vizcom hosted / attended / sponsored {Event} in {place} from {start} to {end}.`
2. `Key conversations centered on {one-line observation, grounded in a specific exchange}.`
3. `{Contact} is available to discuss; recap at {blog URL}.`

**Contact mapping:** Sophia Silver for product-focused events; Kim Lu for community/creator; Jordan Taylor for keynotes. (See `core_rules.md` Section 9.)

## `summary.md` notes

- Pre-event sequence: blog T-7 days, LinkedIn T-5, X T-3, reminder X T-1.
- Post-event sequence: standard same-day.
- Voice self-check: events tempt the agent into exclamation points ("Join us!"). Strip these.
