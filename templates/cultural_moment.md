# Template — Cultural Moment

**signal_type =** `cultural_moment`.

A broader event Vizcom can connect to: major design exhibition, manufacturing-anniversary, craft-vs-AI debate, year-end retrospective, industry awards season. Register is reflective, not promotional.

Channel-length / hashtag / emoji rules live in `core_rules.md`.

## `blog.md`

### H1 patterns
- `{The cultural moment}: A note from Vizcom`
- `Why {moment} matters for {industrial design / footwear / etc.}`
- `{Moment-specific noun phrase}` (e.g., "The return of the prototype")

### Dek
One sentence connecting the moment to designers and the work.

### Body H2 outline

```
[Hook — the moment itself. Place + time. Specific.]

## The moment
(2–3 sentences naming what's happening and why it matters now.)

## Why designers should care
(3–4 sentences grounding the moment in design practice. Cite specific designers / schools / studios when the signal includes them.)

## Vizcom's view
(3–5 sentences. Restrained register. Use the "amplifying the human spark behind every object in the world" line only if the moment earns it.)

## How designers in the community are responding
(Optional. Include if the signal references specific community responses.)

## (optional) A note from {founder / advocate}
```

### Closing line precedents
- `Design is the art form of our age.`
- `Every sketch is the beginning of a legacy — yours to shape.`

### Template-specific voice rules
- Restraint. This template is most at risk of slipping into Sample 10's 2024 mythic register. Stay in 2025–2026 register.
- Specific cultural references welcome (László Almásy / Pre-Columbian artifacts / "Thing" from Addams Family) when grounded in the signal.
- Avoid filler phrases: "this moment" / "this era" / "in this moment."

## `summary.md` notes

- Cultural moments rarely produce a sharp posting time — suggest date-of-the-moment-or-day-after, not the standard 0/15m/30m sequence.
- Voice self-check: restraint, no mythic register, ground every claim in a specific designer or workflow.
